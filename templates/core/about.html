About Helpii

Your trusted courier partner across New Zealand

Our Story

Helpii was founded with a simple mission: to provide fast, reliable, and affordable courier services across New Zealand.

Since our inception, we've grown to become one of the most trusted names in the logistics industry, delivering thousands of parcels every month to satisfied customers nationwide.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional courier services that exceed customer expectations through innovation and dedication.

Our Vision

To be New Zealand's leading courier service, known for reliability, speed, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, customer focus, and continuous improvement in everything we do.
